Privacy Policy for DOs

Last updated: March 2026

DOs respects your privacy.

Information We Collect

DOs does not collect personal data.

Tasks, routines, reminders, streaks, and productivity data are stored locally on your device using Apple’s system storage.

The developer does not operate user accounts and does not store your data on external servers.

Local Device Storage

All productivity data created within DOs remains on your device unless you choose to back up your device using Apple services such as iCloud.

The developer does not have access to your stored data.

You are responsible for maintaining device backups where applicable.

Notifications

If notifications are enabled, reminder scheduling is handled locally on your device through Apple’s notification system.

Notification delivery depends on device configuration and operating system behavior.

The developer does not receive notification usage data.

Subscriptions

If you subscribe to DOs Premium, purchases are processed securely by Apple through the App Store.

The developer does not receive access to your payment information.

Only confirmation of subscription status is provided by Apple.

Analytics

DOs does not currently use analytics, tracking technologies, or third-party monitoring services.

If anonymous analytics are introduced in future versions of the app, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

Data Sharing

DOs does not sell, share, transfer, or disclose user data to third parties.

Children’s Privacy

DOs does not knowingly collect information from children.

International Users

Because DOs stores data locally on your device, your information remains under your control regardless of your geographic location.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as the app evolves.

Updates will be reflected on this page.
