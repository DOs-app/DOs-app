Privacy Policy for DOs

Last updated: March 2026

DOs respects your privacy.

Information We Collect

DOs does not collect personal data.

All tasks, routines, reminders, and productivity data are stored locally on your device using Apple’s system storage.

DOs does not operate user accounts and does not store your data on external servers.

Notifications

If notifications are enabled, reminder scheduling is handled locally on your device through Apple’s notification system.

DOs does not receive notification usage data.

Purchases

If you subscribe to DOs Premium, payments are processed securely by Apple through the App Store.

DOs does not receive access to your payment information.

Only confirmation of subscription status is received from Apple.

Analytics (Future Use)

DOs may introduce anonymous analytics in the future to improve app performance and features.

If analytics are introduced:

  • they will not identify you personally
  • they will not track activity outside the app
  • this policy will be updated accordingly

Data Sharing

DOs does not sell, share, or transfer user data to third parties.

Children’s Privacy

DOs does not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as the app evolves.

Updates will be reflected on this page.
